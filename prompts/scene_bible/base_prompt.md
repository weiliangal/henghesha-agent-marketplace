# Scene Bible Prompt

Describe the scene as a reusable production asset.

Required outputs:

- `scene_id`
- `location`
- `time_of_day`
- `lighting_mood`
- `camera_language`
- `ambient_activity`
- `blocking_notes`
- `continuity_constraints`
- `safe_variation_range`

Rules:

- Keep the scene consistent across reshoots and bridge shots.
- Make background and lighting easy to reproduce.
